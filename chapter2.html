In this project we will be programming in C#. We are going to need an IDE to code, and I suggest using Visuo Studios. There are lots of information online on how to code in C#, and you can look at my playlist to get caught up.

Scripts are crucial parts to UNity. This is how our sprits or assets interat with each other. Without them, it would be like we were just painting a picture, not creating a video game.
